Mission

To meet the alternate energy requirements of customers through reliable, environment-friendly and sustainable supply of Liquefied Petroleum products, while conducting company business profitably, professionally, efficiently, ethically and with responsibility to all our community and the nation.

About Us

A Private limited company, incorporated under the laws of Pakistan established in 2006 with its registered office address Industrial Estate, Dhoda Road Kohat, KPK is fully owned is the Pakistan LPG marketing company. The company has had LPG Storage and filling plant located at Industrial Estate Dhoda road Kohat. With storage 120 Metric tonne capacity. M/S Kotal Gas Pvt.Ltd having 04 LPG Vehicles. As safety is our priority and LPG plant operation is particular that is why to carry out daily operation at plant we have employed qualified staff having relevant experience in the particular field.

Our Services

Kotal Gas provides LPG for domestic use in 11.8 kg and 15 kg cylinders. it’s come complete with cylinder and regulator. Kotal Gas authorized distributors are spread across Pakistan. Company has its own dedicated LPG bottling plants to ensure smooth and continous supply of LPG to its customers. Each cylinder is individually sealed to ensure that right quantity and quality of gas reaches to its customer. Kotal Gas provide 45.4 kg cylinder for this segment. These cylinders can be used individually or can be connected to a manifold system.

Kotal Gas shares the common ambition of becoming the LPG Company of choice for consumers/Distributor within the area in which they operate. To achieve this, they are encouraged to put the customer at the heart of the local companies’ commercial strategy, thereby ensuring that Gasman customers are delivered a world class sustainable service.
